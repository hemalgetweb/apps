FAQs

Digital Marketing Services in Dubai

Here are some of frequently asked questions about digital marketing.

  • Why should I hire Wadi AL Bada when there are so many other digital marketing companies in Dubai?

    Yes, there is no lack of digital marketing service providers in the UAE. But not every digital agency in Dubai has the expertise, experience, creative team composition, marketing tools, and other related facilities to serve the different types and sizes of business enterprises in this gulf state.

  • How can you enhance my brand value?

    We can develop a comprehensive digital marketing strategy that aligns with your business objectives. This strategy will involve identifying your target audience, understanding their needs and preferences, and creating a plan to reach them through various digital channels.

    Not only that, we will create high-quality content (e.g. blogs, social media posts, videos, and other forms of content) to establish your brand as an authority in your industry and engage with your target audience.

    What’s more, we will audit your business website and app to look for new scopes to enhance the overall user experience and fix any existing issues. All of these endeavors will help you enhance your brand value by creating a strong digital presence, engaging with your target audience, and establishing your brand as a thought leader in your industry.

  • What type of clients have you worked with so far?

    With years of industry-expertise, we have had the opportunity to work with a number of Dubai-based companies that include startups, SMEs (small and medium enterprises), and large conglomerates.

    However, most of our projects revolve around these sectors - travel and tourism, healthcare, eCommerce, banking, education, food and restaurants, social, game, entertainment, and lifestyle.

  • How much do you charge for your services?

    We offer a range of digital marketing and design and development services to get your business up and running online. That means we have got you covered from every aspect as the best website designer in Dubai, from designing and developing a website and an app for your business to launching them and applying SEO, PPC and other marketing tactics to generate leads, increase sales, and make every venture a success.

    Since different clients have different business objectives, we recommend that you get in touch with us and share your needs so we can better understand and create a custom package for you.

  • Do you provide real-time customer support?

    Yes, of course. We have a 24/7 customer support team ready to answer your queries.

  • How will you keep me informed of the progress?

    Based on the type of service you have signed up for, we will assign a dedicated project manager and a team that will work on your project ground up. You will have direct contact with the project manager who will keep you updated on a regular basis throughout the service cycle.

  • How do you run a digital marketing project?

    There are several steps involved here.
    Initial Consultation: First we arrange a meeting to discuss your goals, expectations, and budget so we can understand your requirements and recommend the most suitable services.
    Proposal and Agreement: After the initial consultation, we provide a detailed proposal outlining the services to be provided, timelines, and costs. Once you approve the proposal, we sign an agreement to initiate the project.
    Onboarding: In this stage, we gather all the necessary information from you, such as brand guidelines, social media accounts details, website credentials, and any other relevant information needed to execute the project.
    Strategy Development: Based on the information gathered, we develop a comprehensive digital marketing strategy tailored to your goals and objectives.
    Implementation: Then we start executing the digital marketing strategy across multiple channels, such as social media, search engines, email marketing, and other relevant platforms.
    Monitoring and Analysis: We closely monitor the performance of every marketing campaign and analyze the data to optimize the strategies for better results.
    Reporting: We provide regular reports to you outlining the performance of the campaign, including key metrics such as traffic, engagement, and conversions.
    Ongoing Support: We continue to provide after-sale assistance, including making updates and adjustments to the strategy according to the performance and feedback.

  • How do you accept payments?

    We have hourly and fixed rates that come in the form of packages for our services. We usually accept payments through PayPal, stripe, payoneer, and wire transfer.
