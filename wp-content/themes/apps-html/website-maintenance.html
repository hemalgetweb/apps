FAQs

Ask Your Questions & Get Factual Responses

Here are the most common questions we get asked by business owners in the UAE and abroad. If you cannot find the answer to your question below, feel free to contact us.

  • How do you access my website to provide website maintenance services?

    We will ask you to provide us with your website access details as soon as you will sign up for our web maintenance services.

  • Will my website be down when you do your maintenance work?

    It depends on the type of maintenance work being performed on your website. If it requires your website to be taken offline or certain features to be temporarily disabled, then your site may be down during that time. However, if the maintenance work is being done on the backend of your website or in a way that does not affect the front-end user experience, then there will be no downtime issues. We will definitely inform you if we need to temporarily shut down your website in advance and take the necessary measures so it does not impact your business negatively.

  • Can I see your changes before you make them live to make sure I get the chance to approve?

    In case of WordPress websites, changes are usually made to the live site. But if you would like to review them before they go live, we can create a development domain and move a copy of your website to it so that you can check and approve them before we deploy the updated development domain to the live domain.

  • What is the website maintenance cost in Dubai?

    The cost of website maintenance can vary depending on the type and the level of services required. The major factors that contribute to the cost are the size and complexity of your website, the frequency of updates, the type of content being updated, and the level of security needed. Generally, basic website maintenance services like software updates, backups, and security checks may cost between $200 to $500 per year, while more comprehensive services like content updates, SEO optimization, and custom development may cost $1,000 to $5,000 per year or even more.

  • Does web maintenance help with SEO rankings?

    Yes, definitely. Regular web maintenance ensures that your website is functioning correctly, up-to-date, and free of errors, which positively impact your SEO rankings. Some of the mandatory website maintenance services in Dubai include regularly updating your content, fixing broken links, improving site speed, and optimizing images.

  • What do website maintenance services include?

    The range of services offered by a website maintenance agency can vary depending on the needs of the website but here are some common website maintenance services:

    Backup and restore: Regularly backing up the website's data and files, and restoring them in case of any data loss or website crashes.

    Security updates: Installing and updating the site's security software and plugins to protect against malware, hacking, and other security threats.

    Website speed optimization: Analyzing and optimizing website performance to ensure fast loading speed and optimal user experience.

    Website analytics: Regularly tracking and analyzing web traffic and user behavior using tools like Google Analytics.

    Bug fixing:

    Identifying and fixing bugs, errors, and broken links on the site.

    Domain and hosting management: Managing website domain registration, hosting, and renewal.

    Design updates: Updating website design, layout, and functionality to keep up with the current trends and user preferences.

    Search engine optimization (SEO): Implementing SEO strategies to help improve website visibility and search engine rankings.
